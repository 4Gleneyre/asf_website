Alternative Fuels

Curtailing dependence on oil, and switching to alternative renewable fuels is crucial to the survival of the planet. Both Ford and GM have alternative fuel vehicles in production. ASF encourages the production of alternative fuel vehicles as well as the cooperation of the oil companies to make the necessary conversion; to produce, distribute, and vend these new fuels at all filling stations.

ASF seeks to deploy an alternative fuel division to research and develop alternatives to petroleum products. Additionally, due to the great multitude of gasoline operated vehicles extant, ASF seeks to explore the potentials of aftermarket products that modify the molecular structure of gasoline, and or, engine modifications that can radically increase gas mileage and vehicle performance. ASF invites the participation of concerned parties with innovative technologies and requests that they contact ASF.

In 2019 – US Congress has passed the Farm Bill legalizing industrial hemp. Industrial hemp makes numerous products, including fuel, plastics, housing and more! Hemp has promising prospects for the automotive industry.
