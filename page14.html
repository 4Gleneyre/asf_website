Volunteers

Help make the roads safer by volunteering with Automobile Safety Foundation.

Greetings,

Thank you for your interest in volunteering to help the good cause of auto safety…on the road we all copilot.

ASF has international life-saving information at, carsafe.org a Google Ads Grantspro Grant ($40,000 a month in Google Ads) and valuable auto  safety IP. 

Over a million people die every year in auto accidents and the auto is the number one cause of harm and death to students!   That is why students, and everyone, should get behind the World Safety Drive carsafe.org or the SWSD, http://carsafe.org/swsd/ featuring the Copilot Club, offering road concentration, and passenger safety training free for everybody worldwide! Here are ways you can help: 

Currently, ASF is seeking tech skills such as: 

a) Wordpress maintenance, or site redesign (requires demos). 

b) Creating auto safety videos (Copilot Club is very popular on the Ad grant, and is perfect for an app/videosince it has functions…Traffic, Weather, Rideshare 

c) Fundraising. Utilize social networking, grant writing or other fundraising work. Volunteers can work pro bono or (optionally) as ASF cause marketing partner see here/@10 percent of funds raised by you  (with a future VP offer). 

d) Business development, and or, marketing, promotion, secretarial: social networking etc. promoting World Safety Drive/SWSD: Copilot Club carsafe.orgto all college newspapers, web pages, blogs etc. also, check out some merchandising recently completed, https://www.cafepress.com/worldsafetydrive (perhaps you can market this).

Your original ideas that can aid auto safety such as worthwhile ideas for WSD World Safety Drive etc.

e) Google Ads certified partner skills to help manage the ASF Google GrantsPro ($40,000 a month) Ad grant.

Community Service: ASF is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that offers community service that allows you to work from your location and make your own hours, on tech work that is based on fair market value per hours/work. ASF can furnish a community service letter per completion (Courts allow remote tech work, you may want to double-check this with your Court). 

Please study the ASF web page, www.carsafe.org, and then respond here with your proposal including information on how you would apply your skills to the ASF safety work? Since public safety work requires quick execution, please be ready to start right away (within 24 hours of applying and the ASF acceptance) so when responding, also include how soon you could start and an ETA for completion of your work, as it also is important that you are certain you have the time to complete the work shortly after starting it (this applies to WordPress work, long-range projects do not have this limitation). Be concise about the skills you are offering, when you can start, and your ETA for completion 

Please email info@carsafe.org with your information and additionally feel free to leave phone contact info. 

Thank you!
