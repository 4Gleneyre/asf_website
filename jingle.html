ASF Driving Safety Jingle

World's #1 Safety Jingle
Eyes on the Road - Hands on the Wheel

Distracted driving is the international number one cause of harm and death!  "Eyes on the Road - Hands on the Wheel" is the world's #1 driving safety jingle that can save lives globally!  Make roads safer with TV and radio airplay.  Share this video with family, friends, employees, and everyone!
